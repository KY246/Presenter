Presenter is a custom made Power Point program. This was made by a beginner high school programmer, because he does not like how the official slide show programs doesn't have scrolling for large texts in the slideshow, and wanted a better slide show program with more options and flexibility. Although this is still in its early stages of development, the idea is that anything that can be done with HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript (ES6) can be done on this slide show. That also includes companion languages, such as WebGL, GLSL, and others, which will allow users (himself) to use fast and glitch-free 3D, as well as numerous other helpful tools. Another thing that is useful about having this flexibility, is that he can add user interactions, special animations, and if desired, he can even stick a small game or quiz into every slide.
